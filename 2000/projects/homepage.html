homepage

You are browsing it right now ;-) After I decided what my CV should be available online, I've got a good reason to build personal homepage. I am glad about my decision! It is real fun to build your web page. It is so interesting, what I am writing this text at 4:31am, 2000/05/28. I'm working on the page since 17:00pm, 2000/05/27 with short "technical" interruptions ;-) Probably I am so restless because of good Jim Morrison's music.

Design concepts used during building:

  • lightweighted html and graphics for small download times;
  • MetaCreations-like visual style: variety of colors, Verdana font and table-based alignment;
  • two levels of hierarcy, clean and short URLs (as it is recomended by Artem Lebedev)
  • no sounds at all: no wav, no rm, no mid, no mod, not even au ;-)

May be some day I will get rich and buy a brand new "doba.org" domain for this homepage. As of 2000/05/28 It is still not reserved...
