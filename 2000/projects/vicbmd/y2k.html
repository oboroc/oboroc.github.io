Year 2000 compliance


I. VICTORIABANK has recognized that Year 2000 compliance is a critical issue for Bank's business continuity after Year 2000. We are working in co-operation with our suppliers and using all our available resources to ensure that our services will not be affected by the Year 2000 event. A special project - Year 2000 Project was established in June 1998.

II. Year 2000 Project is supervised by Vice-Chairman of the Board. Several top managers are selected for monitoring the project in the Steering Committee:

  • IT Department Manager,
  • Vice-Director, responsible for Loan Department & Policy,
  • Chief Accountant, Manager of Accounting & Reports Department,
  • Manager of International Affairs & ForEx Department,
  • Manager of Securities & Investment Section,
  • Deputy Manager of Credit Card Section.

III. For duties of the Project Manager is assigned IT Department Manager.

IV. Project Phases & Milestones:

  • Project organization - completed.
  • Software and equipment inventory - completed.
  • Detailed analysis from Y2K point of view - permanently.
  • Risk analysis and priorities establishment - completed.
  • Resource allocation for systems and applications - completed.
  • Reoperation of detected problems - completed.
  • Testing of fixed applications - completed.

V. Year 2000 compliance status:

  • 1. Victoriabank has at the moment Y2K compliant main systems (i.e. banking applications - general ledger, loan application, SWIFT interface, etc.).
  • 2. For secondary systems we have to finish the last test before November 31, 1999.

VI. As about the main systems - we are in process of implementation of a new integrated banking solution - EQUATION/3 from Midas-Kapiti International. Manufacturer certified this product for Y2K compliance.

Other systems was tested, corrected and adapted in accordance with following procedures:

  • 1. System analysis;
  • 2. Development of ammendments and fixes to the system;
  • 3. Testing;
  • 4. Implementation of Y2K compliant subsystems.

The third parties systems are being carefully tested to ensure compliance with Year 2000 before their implementation.

VII. The staff is being informed about internal and external progress regarding the Year 2000 Project.

VIII. Our main suppliers have been asked to provide us confirmation certificates for Y2K compliance.

IX. We started procedures to inform our customers about the Bank's preparation/readiness to face Year 2000.

X. According to the recommendations of the National Bank of Moldova, we have tested all systems, provided by NBM, in established terms.
