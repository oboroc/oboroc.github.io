Report of the Board of Directors


Victoriabank, one of the first commercial banks established in Moldova, celebrated its ninth anniversary in 1998. The Bank was established on December 22, 1989. During the past years, the Bank has become a serious financial institute. It has confidently dominated the Moldovan market and plays a leading role in the banking system of the Republic. Also, during the years, the Bank has improved its financial indicators, which increases the confidence in the Bank.

The stable development of the Bank's activities attracted the attention of foreign investors. In December 1995 Victoriabank was reorganized into a Bank with foreign capital. As at the December 31, 1998 64% of the Bank's shares are held by foreign investors (the European Bank for Reconstruction and Development (15%), SPAREX (Belgium) holding 20%, the Danube Investment Fund (Greece / 17%), Intrade Management Limited (England) - 10%, LR-Uniune holding 8%, ORATEH (Moldova) holding 3%, Notabil-Polis (Moldova) holding 1.8%, Engineering Production Company (Moldova) holding 1.5% and Liana International Products (USA) holding 1.2%.

As at December 31, 1998 shareholders equity amounted to Lei 40.9 million including the shareholders' capital of Lei 18 million, that increased by 16% in comparison to the year 1997.

The total assets of the Bank increased by Lei 54.7 million (31%) and interest-bearing assets increased up to Lei 127.6 million. As at December 31, 1998, the capital adequacy ratio equaled to 23%, above the National Bank of Moldova's required minimum of 10%. Net income after tax amounts to Lei 10.2 million (Lei 1.15 million more than in 1997).

Victoriabank actively supports the development of private businesses. The credit lines received from the EBRD (USD 5 million), from the International Bank for Reconstruction and Development (USD 2 million), as well as other resources, made it possible to grant Lei 45 million additional loans to these businesses. The total loan portfolio now amounts to Lei 133.8 million.

Victoriabank is the only bank in Moldova that has the Principal Member status of VISA International and Europay/MasterCard. Today, about 30 locations exist in Chisinau that facilitate credit card payments. Currently Five ATM's are owned of which three are operational.

Entering into 1999, the Bank will use its efforts to continue the adopted policies. The increased needs for financial development of private businesses obliges us to search for new credit resources and to strengthen business and partnership relations with our correspondent hanks. The main item of the Bank's policy remains to provide our clients with high quality services. The Bank believes it can achieve these goals by increasing the efficiency in all the departments by implementing new banking technologies and by enlarging the technical infrastructure.

The Board expresses that it considers the stable and reliable image it has as utmost importance and it will continue to operate in such a way that the Bank's image remains unharmed.


Victor Turcanu
Chairman of the Board
