Victoriabank Web Site

I built the very first version of Victoriabank web site in 1999. Most work went into two large html files with annual reports for 1997 and 1998. As of May 2000, many parts of the site are still as they were designed initially and lots of things have changed. The site is now JavaScript-powered. It supports three different languages (English/Romanian/Russian) instead of English-only. A mirror of initial version I built is available here. Current Victoriabank web site is available here.

I worked in JSC Victoriabank from the July 1997 to January 2000. At the begining of 1999 my manager asked me to build bank's first web site. Since I had zero experience with html and graphics design, I hired a professional graphics designer do site logos and purchased a very good "Web Master n a Nutshell" book by O'Reilly and Associates (1996, ISBN 1-56592-229-8). Graphics designer vectorized logo from one of the print brochures and proposed several mock-ups. I chose fourth or fifth and started to work on web site content.
