Victoriabank Web Site

Victoriabank Web Site is accessible via http://www.victoriabank.md URL. For this page I developed html layout and wrote two big html files: Annual Report for Years 1997 and 1998. Lots of things are kept as they was designed initially, and lots of things have been changed. The site is now JavaScript-powered. And it supports three different languages (English/Romanian/Russian) instead of English-only. Initial version, developed by me is available in zip file here.

I worked in JSC Victoriabank from the Summer, 1997, until January, 2000. At the begining of 1999 my manager, very kind and understanding man, asked me to build Bank's Web Site project. Since I was almost a zero both in HTML layout and graphics design, I applied to a professional graphics designer for site logos and purchased a very good "Web Master in a Nutshell" book by O'Reilly and Associates (1996, ISBN 1-56592-229-8). Graphics designer re-engined form paper-print Victoriabank's logotype into vectorial form and proposed several web-site design concepts. I chosed fourth or fifth and started to work out the web site.
