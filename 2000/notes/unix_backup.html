Backup under UNIX

Install/configure rsh or ssh. When use something like this: 

# tar cvf  - `cat /home/BACKUP/files.lst` | rsh remote_host dd\
 obs=20b of=/dev/rmt0

or with compression 

# tar cvf  - `cat /home/BACKUP/files.lst` | \
gzip -c | \
rsh remote_host dd obs=20b of=/dev/rmt0

To restore: 

# rsh remote_host dd if=/dev/rmt0 ibs=20b | \
gzip -dc | tar xvf -
