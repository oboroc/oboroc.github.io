SWIFT

Introduction

SWIFT is an international bank network, designed for secure and reliable financial messaging. You can find a lot of information at SWIFT's Web Site. I am working as a SWIFT software and hardware administrator since 1997, and I would like to say what SWIFT engineers are very smart, and SWIFT helpdesk is the best helpdesk I ever meet in my life.
If you are living in ex-USSR, you may want to contact Alliance Factors - commercial company from Moscow, which provides basic helpdesk services in russian language. But I wouldn't really recomend you to do so. Those guys are hostile for unknown reason and they are not very qualified. But they are really hungry for your Company's money ;-) So you'd better deal directly with people from SWIFT. They are professional, kind and very friendly.
